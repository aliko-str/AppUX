Greetings, Participant!


In this session, we will ask you to evaluate several websites and webpages in a few simple steps:

  1. Fill out a short demographics questionnaire.

  2. Rate a few screenshots of webpages.

  3. Read a scenario. Try imagining yourself in the situation that the scenario describes.
    A Google-like interface will appear with a list of website links and text snippents. Feel free to explore the websites for at least 5 min. (All links will open in new tabs.)
    Re-arrange the list of websites according to what's said in your scenario. All text snippents can be drag&dropped:

  4. For each website, find specific info and answer a few questions about the website.

  5. Describe how you rated websites with three short phrases.

  6. The End.
